Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about home additions

How long does a home addition typically take?

+

Most standard home additions take 8-16 weeks from breaking ground to completion. The timeline depends on the size and complexity of your project. A single-room addition might take 8-10 weeks, while larger projects like second-story additions may extend to 12-16 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase, and our clear communication throughout prevents surprises.

Will my addition look like it was "tacked on"?

+

Absolutely not. Seamless integration is our specialty. We carefully match your existing home's architecture, materials, rooflines, siding, and windows. Our design process ensures your addition feels like it's always been part of your housenot an afterthought. We take pride in creating additions that enhance your entire home's aesthetic.

Will a home addition increase my property value?

+

Yes! Home additions consistently offer strong returns on investment. Adding square footage, especially functional spaces like bedrooms, bathrooms, or primary suites, significantly boosts property value and buyer appeal. Most homeowners recoup 50-80% of their investment at resale, and you enjoy the benefits immediately. Plus, you avoid the costs and stress of moving to a larger home.

Do I need to move out during construction?

+

In most cases, no. We take great care to minimize disruption to your daily life. We'll work with you to establish clear work zones, maintain clean job sites, and schedule noisy work during convenient times. For most additions, you can remain comfortable in your home. We'll discuss any special considerations during the planning phase.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

+

Yes, absolutely. We handle all permits, building codes, zoning requirements, and inspections. Our team is well-versed in local regulations and maintains excellent relationships with building departments. This is part of our turnkey serviceyou don't have to navigate the bureaucracy. We ensure everything is done correctly and legally.
